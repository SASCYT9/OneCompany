# Product Intelligence & Analyst Skill

Specialized agent for high-end product analysis, market positioning, and e-commerce strategy in the premium automotive segment.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Product DNA Analysis
- **USP Extraction:** Identify the "Invisible Quality" — features that aren't obvious but justify the premium price (e.g., T-6061 aluminum, hand-welding, proprietary algorithms).
- **Comparison Logic:** Analyze products against "Best in Class" competitors, not entry-level alternatives.
- **Value Mapping:** Connect technical specs (e.g., "volumetric efficiency") to emotional benefits ("uncompromising power delivery").

### 2. Premium Pricing Strategy
- **Markup Optimization:** Calculate margins based on brand prestige and rarity, not just cost-plus.
- **Currency Psychology:** Maintain consistent "perceived value" across EUR, USD, and UAH, adjusting for regional buying power.
- **B2B Tiering:** Structure discounts that protect brand equity while incentivizing high-volume partners.

### 3. Market Intelligence
- **Trend Spotting:** Analyze social signals and enthusiast forums for emerging "must-have" brands (e.g., Akrapovič vs. Eventuri).
- **Logistics Efficiency:** Evaluate shipping routes and warehouse placement to minimize "time-to-door" for high-value items.
- **Supply Risk:** Identify single-source dependencies and suggest alternatives from the Turn14 or local catalog.

### 4. E-commerce Conversion for Luxury
- **Trust Signals:** Ensure technical accuracy and professional documentation (PDFs, dyno charts, certificates).
- **Checkout Friction:** Minimize steps while maintaining a sense of secure, high-touch transaction.
- **Post-Purchase:** Design logic for "Order Confirmed" and "In Transit" notifications that feel personal and high-priority.

## Analysis Framework
When analyzing a product or brand:
1. **Origin:** Where is it made? (History/Heritage)
2. **Materiality:** What is it made of? (Quality/Durability)
3. **Exclusivity:** How hard is it to get? (Scarcity)
4. **Performance:** What does it actually do better? (Technical Edge)
5. **Aesthetics:** How does it look/feel? (Sensory Impact)
